# FanLab Marketing Clustering Project
Marketing data clustering project for FanLab using K-Means to segment streamers and generate data-driven strategy recommendations.

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing livestream creator performance data for FanLab using K-Means Clustering to identify distinct streamer segments based on audience engagement, loyalty, community growth, and livestream performance. The analysis was conducted as part of a marketing data analytics final project with the goal of transforming raw creator data into actionable business and marketing strategies.

## Business Background
FanLab is a livestreaming and creator-support platform operating in the creator economy space. As the number of streamers on the platform grows, FanLab needs a better understanding of creator behavior, audience engagement, and community loyalty in order to design more effective platform features, retention programs, and growth strategies.

Rather than treating all streamers as one homogeneous group, this project aimed to segment streamers into meaningful clusters so that FanLab could develop more personalized and data-driven marketing strategies.

## Project Objective
The objective of this project was to segment FanLab streamers based on livestream performance and audience behavior, identify key differences between streamer groups, and translate those insights into strategic recommendations that support creator growth, loyalty, and platform sustainability.

## Analytical Approach
This project used **K-Means Clustering** to group streamers with similar behavioral and performance patterns. The analysis focused on variables related to:
- viewer engagement,
- audience loyalty,
- community growth,
- livestream interaction,
- monetization-related performance.

The optimal number of clusters was determined using the **Elbow Method**, resulting in three main streamer segments.

## Cluster Segmentation Results
The clustering analysis identified **three main streamer segments**:

### 1. Viral Top Performers
A small group of high-performing streamers with the highest viewer reach, likes, watch time, and follower growth. These streamers drive a significant portion of FanLab’s engagement and platform value, but their audience loyalty is only moderate.

### 2. Small Emerging Streamers
The largest segment, consisting of early-stage or lower-performing streamers with relatively low engagement, viewer retention, and monetization performance. Despite weak performance, this segment represents FanLab’s largest growth opportunity.

### 3. Loyal Community Streamers
A segment with the strongest audience loyalty and highest average viewing duration. While they do not generate the same scale of reach as Viral Top Performers, they have more committed communities and stronger long-term engagement quality.

## Key Insights
The analysis generated several important business insights:
- **High viewer volume does not always lead to high loyalty.** Viral Top Performers dominate traffic and engagement metrics, but Loyal Community Streamers show much stronger audience retention and loyalty.
- **Most streamers are still in the early growth stage.** Over 70% of streamers belong to the Small Emerging Streamers cluster, indicating a major opportunity for creator development and platform support.
- **Loyal communities and viral growth are not the same thing.** Some streamers have strong audience retention but struggle to attract new viewers, which suggests the need for different growth strategies across segments.
- **Platform value is highly concentrated among a small number of top creators.** This creates both an opportunity and a business risk for FanLab if the platform becomes too dependent on a small elite group of streamers.

## Strategy Recommendations
Based on the segmentation results, the project proposed several data-driven marketing and product strategies for FanLab, including:

### 1. AI Creator Growth Dashboard
A growth support system for emerging streamers that provides personalized recommendations on content performance, engagement improvement, and audience growth opportunities.

### 2. Loyalty & Community Ecosystem
Retention-focused features such as loyalty rewards, watch streaks, fan rankings, and community missions to strengthen long-term audience engagement.

### 3. AI Viral Content & Community Expansion
AI-based tools to automatically identify highlight moments from livestreams and convert them into short-form content for audience acquisition on external platforms.

### 4. Top Creator Retention Program
Exclusive support, advanced analytics, sponsorship matching, and monetization optimization for top-performing creators to reduce platform dependency risks.

### 5. AI-Powered Creator Journey
A personalized AI recommendation system that tailors growth strategies for creators based on their segment, challenges, and stage of development.
